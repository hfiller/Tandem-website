About US

We want to enable remote developers to be as productive as those in the office
As if you're in the Same Room

Remote development is a growing trend, and it's making developers happier, offices cheaper, and allowing teams to be more international. Already 30% of all developers are working remotely at least part time, and it's time development tools starting making up for this.

Current tools aren't built for remote software collaboration, but Tandem is. We have seen the problems ourselves, and decided to solve them as best we could.

By snipping the code snippets you have trouble with, sharing those problems with your co-workers, and merging in their solutions, we can replace calling someone over to you desk and asking for help with something faster, easier and better.

  • Remote Development

    30% of all developers are remote according to the most recent Stack OverFlow Developer Survey.

  • Problem Solving

    Studies show 50% of development time is spent on debugging activities.

  • Asking for help

    Co-located developers get their questions answered in less than 2/5ths of the time it takes remote developers.
